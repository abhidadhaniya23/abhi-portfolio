xLinks is a social media platform that allows users to share links to their favorite websites, articles, and videos. Users can also follow other users and like and comment on their posts. The app is built with React, Node, Express, MongoDB.

- [xLinks](https://xlinks.netlify.app/)
- [GitHub](https://github.com/abhidadhaniya23/xlinks)

## Features

- User authentication
- Create, edit, and delete posts
- Like and comment on posts
- Follow and unfollow users
- Search for users and posts
- Notifications
- Dark mode
