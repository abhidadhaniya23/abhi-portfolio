## Introduction

Foodie VVN is a web app that provides restaurants, cafes, lounges and tiffin services in vidhyanagar. It is a progressive web app that can be installed on any device.

## Features

- Search for restaurants, cafes, lounges and tiffin services in vidhyanagar.
- Filter restaurants, cafes, lounges and tiffin services in vidhyanagar by cuisine, cost for two, rating, and more.
- Get directions to the restaurant, cafe, lounge or tiffin service in vidhyanagar.
- View the menu of the restaurant, cafe, lounge or tiffin service in vidhyanagar.
